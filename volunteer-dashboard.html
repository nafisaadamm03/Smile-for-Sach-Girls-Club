How to Support Girls Well

• Greet girls warmly and use their names if they’re comfortable
• Model kindness, patience, and encouragement
• Follow facilitator and coordinator instructions
• Ask questions if you’re unsure about anything

• Keep phones away during programming
• Never share photos or information without permission
• Let staff know if a youth seems upset or uncomfortable
• End each night with a quick check-in with the coordinator
