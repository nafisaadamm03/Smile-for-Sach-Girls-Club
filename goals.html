Objectives & Goals

What we want Girls Club to grow into.

Girls Club was created to offer a consistent, trusted space for girls to connect, play, learn, and dream together. Our objectives and goals guide every workshop, circle, and event.

Core Values

What We Stand For

  • Community Connection – rooting everything in local neighbourhood relationships.
  • Empowerment & Confidence – helping girls recognize their power and voice.
