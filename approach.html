Our Approach

Girls Club is built as a third space rooted in restorative justice.

Third Space

Creating a Third Space for Girls

  • Support girls and young women in recognizing their strengths, developing leadership, and building self-worth.
  • Encourage intergenerational learning and collaboration between girls and youth mentors.
  • Offer practical opportunities to build athletic, creative, academic, and leadership skills.
  • Create environments that celebrate identity, belonging, and mutual respect.
