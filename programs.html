Girls Mentorship Program

Programming designed for girls ages 11–17 at the Jean Augustine Centre.

Our Girls Club Mentorship Program is a welcoming space where girls can build confidence, friendships, and skills through sports, arts, wellness, and leadership activities. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

The program is hosted at the Jean Augustine Centre (101 Portland St, Etobicoke, ON M8Y 1B1) and includes welcome events, introductions, team building, icebreakers, and brainstorm sessions to help shape future programming with the girls. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

Who We Serve

Target Audience

Girls Club is open to all girls ages 11–17. We currently run age-based cohorts:

  • 11–14 year olds
  • 15–17 year olds
