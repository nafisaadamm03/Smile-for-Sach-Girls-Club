Empowering Girls 11–17 to Grow, Play, and Lead

Free, inclusive programs for young women in our community.

Our Mission

To create safe, inclusive, empowering spaces where girls can develop confidence, leadership, creativity, and community.

Programs

  • Sports & Confidence: Basketball, badminton, rock climbing.
  • Arts & Creativity: Paint nights, crafts, design workshops.
  • STEM & Skills: Coding, robotics, hands-on learning.
  • Wellness & Leadership: Self-esteem workshops, team building.
