Our Mission

Creating Safe, Joyful Spaces for Girls

Smile for Sache Girls Club is a youth-focused space where girls can show up as themselves, build confidence, and feel supported by mentors who care. We centre community, belonging, and joy in everything we do.

Through weekly programs, workshops, and special events, we help girls explore who they are, try new things, and develop skills that support them in school, at home, and in the future.

Programs

How We Support Our Girls

Sports & Confidence

Basketball, rock climbing, and movement-based games that build teamwork, resilience, and self-belief on and off the court.

Arts & Creativity

Paint nights, crafts, media projects, and design workshops that give girls space to express themselves and explore their interests.

STEM & Skills

Hands-on learning through coding, problem-solving activities, and future-focused workshops that spark curiosity.

Wellness & Leadership

Conversations about boundaries, self-esteem, friendships, and community care, led in a way that feels safe and youth-centered.

Our Story

Inspired by Care, Built for Community

The Girls Club was created to carry forward the spirit of Smile for Sache by focusing directly on girls in our community. We saw a need for a space where Black, Muslim, newcomer, and racialized girls could feel seen, supported, and celebrated.

Every workshop, game, and conversation is rooted in joy and community care. Our goal is to make sure each girl leaves feeling a little more confident, connected, and hopeful about what‚Äôs possible for her future.
